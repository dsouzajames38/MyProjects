ABOUT E-WASTE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Simple Flow

E-waste collection and recycling in India is largely dominated by the unorganized sector, which makes the enforcement of laws very difficult.

Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed, public, digital ledger that is used to record transactions across many computers so that any involved record cannot be altered retroactively, without the alteration of all subsequent blocks.This ledger is shared among all participants, not owned by any, and records events and transactions in a secure, immutable, and irrevocable way.The System keeps a track of an electronic device right from when it is produced to the time when it is discarded as e-waste.
